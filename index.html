Why I Appreciate You

There are so many things I appreciate about you, and most of them come from your genuine kindness. You always put others before yourself, and you never hesitate to help out. Those are the qualities of an awesome person. I appreciate that I can talk to you whenever I need to, and you do not judge. Even the quick conversations we had have stuck with me more than you realize.
